Bands show season floor (p10) to ceiling (p90) from a Monte-Carlo season simulation — games played sampled from historical availability, weeks linked by a measured correlation; the tick is the simulated median, and VORP is built from that same median. Ordering is points above a replacement-level starter, so scarcity is priced in. Tier gaps mark real value cliffs — when your tier empties, don't chase. Rookies (marked R) carry draft-capital cohort priors — wide bands on purpose; see the about page.
